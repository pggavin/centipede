Guide :

Centipede is a classic arcade game released in 1981 by Atari. The player controls a small ship (known as the shooter) at the bottom of the screen and must shoot a centipede as it moves down the screen. The centipede starts at the top of the screen and moves down one row at a time, changing direction when it hits the edge of the screen or a mushroom (which are placed randomly around the playfield). If the player shoots a segment of the centipede, it breaks apart and moves independently, leaving a mushroom behind. You lose if a centipede segment reaches the bottom 2 rows

You don't actually kill a centipede in this you kill a series of evil green balls.
